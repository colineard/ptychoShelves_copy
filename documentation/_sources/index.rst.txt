PtychoShelves documentation
============================


PtychoShelves is a Matlab-based software package, developed by the CXS group [1]_ [2]_, Paul-Scherrer Institut, Switzerland. With its modular framework, it is designed to provide a flexible, yet fast environment for ptychographic data preparation and reconstructions.
